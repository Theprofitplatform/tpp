Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our digital marketing services

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary by service: Google Ads can show results within 7-14 days, SEO typically takes 3-6 months for significant improvements, and social media campaigns show engagement increases within 2-4 weeks. We provide monthly reports to track your progress.

Do you work with businesses outside of Sydney?

While we specialize in Sydney businesses and understand the local market deeply, we also work with businesses across Australia. Our local Sydney expertise gives us unique insights into the Australian market.

What makes you different from other agencies?

We focus exclusively on results-driven marketing with no lock-in contracts. Our team has deep Sydney market knowledge, we provide transparent reporting, and we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Plus, we're Google Partners with certified experts.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

No lock-in contracts required! We believe in earning your business every month through results. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Our goal is to provide such great results that you'll want to continue working with us.

How much should I budget for digital marketing?

It depends on your goals and industry. Generally, businesses should invest 5-10% of revenue in marketing. Our starter packages begin at $2,500/month, with most growing businesses investing $4,500-7,500/month for comprehensive campaigns.

Will I have a dedicated account manager?

Yes! Every client gets a dedicated account manager who understands your business and goals. For Enterprise clients, you'll have priority access and more frequent check-ins. You'll have direct contact details and regular scheduled calls.

Can you help with my existing website?

Absolutely! We can optimize your existing website for better performance, conversions, and SEO. If needed, we can also recommend improvements or complete redesigns to better support your marketing goals.

What reporting do you provide?

We provide comprehensive monthly reports showing key metrics, ROI, campaign performance, and actionable insights. You'll also have 24/7 access to client dashboards to monitor progress in real-time.
