Method 1: From Current Page

  1. Stay on https://business.google.com/locations
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Console
  3. Click the Console tab
  4. Copy and paste this code:
fetch('https://business.google.com/v2/accounts')
    .then(r => r.json())
    .then(d => console.log('Account ID:', d.accounts[0].name.split('/')[1]))
    .catch(e => console.log('Try Method 2'))

Method 2: Check Network Tab

  1. Press F12 (if not already open)
  2. Click the Network tab
  3. Click on your business "The Profit Platform" again
  4. Look for requests in the Network tab
  5. Find one that contains "accounts" in the URL
  6. The account ID will be in format: accounts/XXXXXXXXX

Method 3: Inspect Page Source

  1. Right-click anywhere on the page
  2. Select "View Page Source" or press Ctrl+U
  3. Press Ctrl+F to search
  4. Search for: "accounts/"
  5. You'll find your Account ID after "accounts/"

Once you find a number that looks like: 12345678901234567890

That's your Account ID!
