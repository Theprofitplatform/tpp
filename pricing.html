Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and services

Do you require long-term contracts?

No, we don't believe in locking our clients into long-term contracts. All our services are month-to-month, and you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What's included in the Google Ads management fee?

Our management fee covers strategy, setup, optimization, monitoring, reporting, and ongoing campaign improvements. The actual ad spend is separate and paid directly to Google.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. You can expect to see initial improvements in 2-3 months, with significant results typically appearing in 4-6 months. We provide monthly reports to track progress.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Absolutely! You can change your package at any time. We'll work with you to find the best solution for your evolving business needs.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes, we create custom packages for businesses with specific needs or larger budgets. Contact us to discuss a tailored solution for your business.

What happens if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our services. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll refund your investment and part ways amicably.
